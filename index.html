About Foundation

Koc University, founded in 1993 in Istanbul with the support of Koc Holding, is one of Turkey's leading private universities, offering a wide range of programs across disciplines such as Business Administration, Engineering, Social Sciences, Law, Media and Communication, and Medicine, all taught in English, with a strong emphasis on academic excellence, research, and close industry ties.

About Vehbi Koc

Vehbi Koc was a prominent Turkish businessman and philanthropist, best known for founding Koc Holding, one of the largest and most successful conglomerates in Turkey. Born in 1901, he played a key role in the development of the Turkish economy, with interests spanning various industries such as automotive, energy, finance, and retail. Koc was also known for his charitable contributions and efforts to improve education and healthcare in Turkey. He passed away in 1996.

Double major and Minor

Koc University offers strong opportunities for **double major** and **minor** programs, allowing students to explore multiple fields of study and broaden their academic horizons. Through the **double major** program, students can pursue two degrees simultaneously, while the **minor** program enables them to gain expertise in an additional subject without the full requirements of a second degree. Both options are designed to provide flexibility and encourage interdisciplinary learning, enhancing students' skills and preparing them for diverse career paths. These opportunities are supported by Koc University's academic structure and personalized advising, ensuring that students can successfully manage their academic workload while pursuing their interests in multiple disciplines.
