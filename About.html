About Foundation

Koc University, founded in 1993 in Istanbul with the support of Koc Holding, is one of Turkey's leading private universities, offering a wide range of programs across disciplines such as Business Administration, Engineering, Social Sciences, Law, Media and Communication, and Medicine, all taught in English, with a strong emphasis on academic excellence, research, and close industry ties.

Mission and Vision

Koc University’s mission is to generate knowledge for the benefit of science, technology, and society, while offering high-quality education that prepares students to become effective leaders and entrepreneurs. Its vision is to be a globally recognized institution, leading in innovation, research, and academic excellence, providing students with the skills to succeed in a competitive world.

International Relations

Koc University’s Department of International Relations focuses on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of global politics, economics, and international relations. The program combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for careers in diplomacy, international organizations, and global affairs. It emphasizes critical thinking, cross-cultural understanding, and global citizenship.

Academic Staff

Koç University’s academic staff is composed of highly qualified faculty members, many of whom have earned their degrees from prestigious universities around the world. The faculty is known for its expertise in various fields, a commitment to research excellence, and a strong focus on student-centered teaching. They actively contribute to international research projects and maintain close ties with global academic networks.
